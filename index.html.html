Common Questions

Everything you need
to know.

Do I need to complete Level 1 before Level 2?
Level 2 assumes working knowledge of Python and basic data analysis. If you are already comfortable with Pandas, EDA, and basic statistics, you can start at Level 2 directly. If you are unsure, take the Level 1 free preview modules to assess your readiness.
How long does each level take to complete?
Level 1 is 45 hours, Level 2 is 50 hours, and Level 3 is 55 hours. Studying part-time at 5 to 8 hours per week, most students complete each level in 6 to 10 weeks. You set your own pace — there are no weekly deadlines.
What software and tools will I need?
All tools used in the course are free and open source: Python (Anaconda distribution), Jupyter Notebooks, VS Code, and GitHub. Power BI Desktop is free to download. Full setup instructions are provided before each relevant module begins.
Are the certificates recognised by employers?
Libra Scale World certificates are issued with a public verification URL and LinkedIn integration. They carry the credibility of Dr. Okonkwo's international profile and are grounded in rigorous, assessed learning. Level 3 certificates additionally note expert panel review, which is rare in online education.
Is there a corporate or team pricing option?
Yes. We offer team licences for organisations wishing to upskill 5 or more staff. Corporate packages include custom cohort scheduling, progress dashboards for HR teams, and optional on-site workshop components. Contact us for a tailored quote.
What happens if I do not pass an assessment?
All assessments can be retaken. There is no penalty for retaking, and you will receive written feedback after each submission. The pass mark of 70% is firm, but we want you to succeed and the course includes revision resources and instructor support throughout.
